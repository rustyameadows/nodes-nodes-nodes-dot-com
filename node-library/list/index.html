List Block

What it does

  • Organizes options, batches, and repeated items.
  • Makes structured ideas easier to read than a wall of text.
  • Gives the next step something cleaner to work from.

Use it when

  • You need a shot list, prompt list, or batch.
  • You want to compare options row by row.
  • A project is starting to sprawl and needs shape.

Works well with

Text notes, templates, and model blocks.

Best for

Options, batches, repeated units, and rows that need to stay aligned.

Why it stays on the canvas

It turns scattered thinking into something you can act on.

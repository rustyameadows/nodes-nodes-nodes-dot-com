List

The List block is for ideas that make more sense as rows, options, batches, or grouped pieces.

Back to library Get the early alpha
List placeholder

What it helps you do

  • Collect options in a shape that is easy to scan.
  • Organize repeated ideas without turning them into a wall of text.
  • Set up batches you may want to send into the next step.

When you would use it

  • When you are comparing ideas side by side.
  • When you want a shot list, a prompt list, or a batch of variations.
  • When you need structure without making things feel rigid.

Often used with

Text notes, templates, and model blocks.

Good for

Options, categories, batches, and repeated pieces of work that benefit from staying aligned.

Why it matters

A good list can turn a vague idea into something you can actually act on.

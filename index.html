Very early Mac alpha

A calmer way to work with AI on your Mac.

Nodes Nodes Nodes keeps prompts, runs, outputs, and review in one project space, so your work is easier to follow from the first idea to the next pass.

See the early alpha Browse the Node Library

Mac only for now. Single-user. Available through GitHub.

Why it feels different

The whole project stays in view.

Instead of juggling tabs, folders, and half-finished notes, you can keep the setup, the run, and the output together in one place.

Your work stays together

Each project keeps its own canvas, runs, and outputs, so one experiment does not spill into another.

You can see what happened

When a run works or fails, the app gives you a clear place to check instead of leaving you guessing.

Review is part of the flow

Compare results, keep the useful ones, and move into the next pass without losing your thread.

Quick questions

A few useful answers before you try it.

What is Nodes Nodes Nodes?

It is a local-first Mac app for building, running, and reviewing creative AI work in one place.

Is it available right now?

Yes. The current version is a very early alpha that you get through GitHub.

Which providers work today?

OpenAI, Google Gemini, and Topaz.

Does it run in the browser?

No. Right now it is a Mac desktop app.

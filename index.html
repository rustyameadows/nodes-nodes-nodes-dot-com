Quick answers

A few things people will ask right away.

What is Nodes Nodes Nodes?

A local-first Mac app for building, running, debugging, and reviewing AI workflows on an infinite canvas.

Is it available right now?

Yes. It is a very early macOS alpha, and the current handoff lives in the GitHub repo.

What providers are currently in scope?

At a high level: OpenAI, Google Gemini, and Topaz.

Does it run in the browser?

No. It is a desktop app.

Does it support collaboration or cloud sync?

Not today. Right now it is local-first and made for one person at a time.

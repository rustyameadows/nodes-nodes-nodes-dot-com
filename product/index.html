Product

Keep the whole project in one place.

Nodes Nodes Nodes is built for people who want their prompts, model runs, outputs, and review decisions to stay close together instead of scattered across too many tabs and folders.

What it gives you

A simpler shape for messy work.

Projects that stay separate

Each project has its own workspace and outputs, so every idea has a clearer home.

A canvas made from useful blocks

Build with notes, lists, templates, assets, and model blocks instead of flattening everything into chat.

Runs you can actually check

When you send something off, there is a clear place to see what happened next.

Review that stays close to the work

Look through results, compare options, and keep the best parts without losing your place.

Main areas

A few spaces, each doing one clear job.

Projects, canvas, runs, and review

The app gives each part of the job its own place. Start from a project home, build on the canvas, check runs when needed, and review outputs without starting over.

Providers you already know

The current alpha works with OpenAI, Google Gemini, and Topaz, so you can bring your own keys and keep control of the services you use.

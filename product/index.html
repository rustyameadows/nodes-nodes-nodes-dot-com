Product

Okay, what is this thing?

It is a local-first desktop app for people who keep inventing tiny AI systems for themselves and then immediately lose track of them.

A fake canvas shot until the real screenshots are worth showing.

What it feels like

The messy middle stays visible

You can see the notes, the prompt fragments, the assets, and the outputs in one workflow instead of one long blur.

Where it lives

On your Mac, not in some mystery cloud

The product is local-first and single-user right now, which is part of why it feels direct.

The nice parts

A few things the app is trying to do well.

Canvas first

Build with notes, templates, asset pointers, and model nodes inside one connected graph.

Queue included

You can actually see what is running instead of wondering which tab is responsible.

Review built in

Grid, 2-up, and 4-up views make it easier to keep the good stuff and throw away the rest.

App home for making and reopening projects.

Asset review with room to compare outputs instead of hoarding them.

Status

Still early, still useful, still a macOS alpha.

The current version is local-first and single-user. Waitlist and public download flows are still being sorted out.
